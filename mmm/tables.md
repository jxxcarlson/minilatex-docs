# Tables

You can, of course, use verbatim text to make tables:

````
   Element    |    Z
   --------------------
   Altium     |    4/5
   Brazilium  |    7/5
   Certium    |    9/5
````

But better is to use Markdown tables:

|  Element  | Symbol |  Z | A |
| Hydrogen  | H      |  1 | 1.008   |
| Helium    | He     |  2 |  4.0026 |
| Lithium   | Li     |  3 |  6.94   |
| Beryllium | Be     |  4 |  9.0122 |
| Boron     | B      |  5 | 10.81   |
| Carbon    | C      |  6 | 12.011  |
| Nitrogen  | N      |  7 | 14.007  |
| Oxygen    | O      |  8 | 15.999  |
| Flourine  | F      |  9 | 18.998  |
| Neon      | Ne     | 10 | 20.180  |

Like this:

```nolang
|  Element  | Symbol |  Z | A |
| Hydrogen  | H      |  1 | 1.008   |
| Helium    | He     |  2 |  4.0026 |
```
You don't have to line up the columns as in the example.
